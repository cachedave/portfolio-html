The Best Movies According to Avery

My top 3 movies of 2024

Singing in the Rain

Each actor provides show-stopping, stunning performances in this film. From the set design, music, dancing, and plot, they simply do not make films like this anymore!

Tár

Cate Blanchett creates a real, living and breathing universe that is Lydia Tár. This dramatic, nearly psychological thriller offers up stimulating discourse regarding cancel culture, ambition, and double standards in girl-boss ideaologies.

Downtown 81

This film is a gem of '80s NYC subculture. Starring Jean-Michel Basquiat, this movie offers an inside look into the No Wave music scene through Basquiat's lens. Bands like Liquid Liquid, Suicide, Plastics, DNA, and Kid Creole and the Coconuts can be seen and heard in this cult-classic.
